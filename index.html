Bob's Very Punny Bet It All On Black Garlic Burger(Sandwich)

    From "The Bob's Burgers Burger Book"
  1. Ingredients
    • Black Garlic, 1 bulb
    • Salt and Pepper
    • Mayonnaise, 1/2 cup
    • Sriracha
    • Ground Beef, 1 pound
    • Fresh Mozzarella, 1 ball, sliced
    • Baby Spinach
    • Buns, 4 pack
  2. Use a food processor and puree black garlic. Mix with mayo, 1/4 tsp salt, and Sriracha.
  3. Form 4 larger than normal beef patties and season with salt and pepper.
  4. Cook patties based on your preference.
  5. Top patties with mozzarella and cover to melt the cheese.
  6. Build the burger in order:
    • Bottom bun
    • Handful of baby spinach
    • Cheeseburger
    • Schmear of black garlic-Sriracha mayo
    • Top bun
